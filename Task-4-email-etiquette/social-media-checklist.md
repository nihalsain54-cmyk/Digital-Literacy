# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist for College Students

## Do's

-   **Think before posting** -- Make sure your content is respectful and
    appropriate.
-   **Protect your privacy** -- Adjust privacy settings and avoid
    sharing personal information like phone numbers, addresses, or
    passwords.
-   **Verify information before sharing** -- Always fact-check news or
    information to avoid spreading misinformation.
-   **Be respectful to others** -- Communicate politely and avoid
    offensive language or harmful comments.
-   **Build a positive online presence** -- Share useful, educational,
    or creative content that reflects your personality and skills.

## Don'ts

-   **Don't share confidential or sensitive information** -- Avoid
    posting personal details about yourself or others.
-   **Don't engage in cyberbullying** -- Never harass, threaten, or
    insult someone online.
-   **Don't post inappropriate content** -- Avoid content that could
    harm your reputation or future career.
-   **Don't believe or share fake news** -- Always check reliable
    sources before reposting information.
-   **Don't argue aggressively online** -- If disagreements happen, stay
    calm and communicate respectfully.
